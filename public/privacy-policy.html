Customer Privacy

Privacy Policy

We keep customer information limited, protected, and used only for Sushi Box ordering, delivery, support, and service improvement.

Effective date: May 24, 2026

Information We Collect

When you place an order, contact us, send feedback, or sign in, Sushi Box may collect your name, phone number, email address, delivery city, address details, order items, and payment status information.

How We Use Information

  • To process orders and coordinate delivery.
  • To contact you about order confirmation, support, or delivery updates.
  • To improve our products, website, customer experience, and service reliability.
  • To support secure checkout and payment processing.

Payments

Online payments are handled by secure payment providers. Sushi Box does not store card numbers or sensitive payment credentials on this website.

Sharing Information

We only share information when needed to complete your order, support delivery, process payment, comply with legal requirements, or protect Sushi Box and our customers.
