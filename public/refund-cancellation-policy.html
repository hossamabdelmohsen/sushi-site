Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our policy protects food safety, frozen product quality, and transparent customer expectations before and after delivery.

Effective date: May 24, 2026

Food and Frozen Product Returns

Because Sushi Box sells food and frozen products, returns or exchanges are not accepted after delivery, as product safety and frozen condition cannot be guaranteed once handed to the customer.

Cancellations Before Delivery

You may contact Sushi Box as soon as possible to request cancellation before the order is prepared, dispatched, or handed to delivery. Cancellation approval depends on order status and product preparation stage.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If an item arrives damaged, incorrect, or missing, contact us immediately on the same delivery day with your order details and clear photos. Sushi Box will review the case and offer an appropriate solution where applicable.

Refund Timing

Approved refunds, when applicable, are processed through the original payment channel or another agreed method. Processing time may depend on the payment provider or bank.
