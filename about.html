About Sushi Box

We Are Sushi Box

Sushi Box is a modern Asian food supply brand built with a clear vision: to bring premium-quality Asian ingredients and sushi essentials to the Egyptian market at the best possible value.

Premium Asian Ingredients for Egypt

We specialize in sourcing and delivering high-quality raw materials for sushi and Asian cuisine, carefully selected to meet the standards of both professional chefs and passionate home cooks. From seaweed sheets (nori), sauces, noodles, dumplings, and baozi, to a wide range of authentic Asian products, Sushi Box is your trusted destination for everything Asian cuisine.

At Sushi Box, quality is not just a promise — it is our foundation. We are committed to providing top-tier products with consistent taste, freshness, and reliability, all while maintaining competitive pricing that makes premium ingredients accessible to everyone.

We take pride in being one of the first specialized suppliers of Asian food ingredients in the Delta region. Our journey started with a mission to fill a gap in the market, and today, we are growing to serve a wider audience across multiple cities.
